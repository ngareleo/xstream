What Moran Does
EVERYTHING YOUR
LIBRARY DESERVES

A full media management suite built for people who keep their films locally. Profiles, metadata, a cinema-grade player, and a watchlist.

Core
🎬
Profile-Based Libraries

Create named profiles for different directories — "Movies", "TV Shows", "4K Vault". Each profile walks its folder tree and builds your catalog automatically. Add or remove profiles any time.

Discovery
🔍
Automatic Metadata Enrichment

Moran reads filenames and file metadata, queries open-source databases (like TMDB), and pulls in posters, plots, cast, ratings, and genres. Persisted locally alongside your files — no repeated network calls.

Playback
▶️
4K Browser-Native Player

A full-screen player with seek bar, volume, resolution switching (240p → 4K), and a side panel suggesting your next watch. Powered by Media Source Extensions — no plugins required.

Planning
🔖
Watchlist

Search for any film and add it to your watchlist. Items that you already have locally are automatically marked. Track what you want to watch next without opening any streaming service.

Setup in Minutes
HOW IT
WORKS
1
Create a Profile

Give it a name and point it to the folder on your disk where your videos live — e.g. ~/Videos/Movies.

30 seconds
2
Moran Scans & Enriches

Moran walks the directory, reads each filename, queries open databases for metadata, fetches posters and details, and saves everything locally next to your files.

Automatic
3
Browse and Play

Click a film to see its poster, plot, and cast. Hit play for the in-browser player or open in VLC. The player suggests your next watch from your watchlist or a sequel.

Instant
4
Manually Identify Edge Cases

If Moran can't match a file automatically, a "Get More Details" button opens a search so you can identify it yourself. No guesswork.
